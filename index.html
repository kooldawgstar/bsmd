About Us

BS/MD programs are becoming increasingly more competitive because of greater interest in these streamlined pathways to careers in medicine. As students who have⁠ successfully⁠ completed the application process to these programs as well as many other prestigious universities around the nation, we have invaluable advice and materials we can provide to you. Thorough research of BS/MD programs around the country and common factors that enhance the favorability of an application has led us to offer an effective and economic service.



Aside from meeting the academic requirements needed to apply to these programs, applicants must build a captivating narrative to distinguish themselves from the rest of the pool. As such, Road to BSMD offers personalized, flexible, and extensive assistance to guide you through every step of the application process. We hope that you recognize the value of the service that we offer and look forward to hearing from you!

Services

Each package provides a variety of detailed resources and assistance from our trained professional staff to assist you.

School List Review

We provide you with a list of carefully curated, competitive, quality schools with BS/MD programs to apply to

Common App Essay Revisions

We will edit your common app essay and provide revisions to better your essay when applying to colleges

Supplemental Essay Revisions

We will edit and provide revisions to your supplemental essays that cater specifically to each college

Interview Preperation

We will organize mock interviews and test you on sample interview questions to prepare youself for college and program interviews

Resumè Assistance

We will edit and revise your resumè to show your skills and experiences effectively to colleges
