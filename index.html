Why BS/MD?

BS/MD programs greatly reduce the stress of students who are 100% confident that they want to pursue a career in medicine, as there is no need to apply to medical school during undergraduate years. Though different programs have various requirements, the trend is that the average BS/MD student feels less pressure to get a good GPA, MCAT score, or necessary extracurriculars. This allows these students to pursue their true passions, expanding their breadth of knowledge and experience. These programs also tend to have more personalized resources, advisors, and opportunities to ensure an enriched pre-medical experience. In the case of accelerated programs, the student saves on tuition costs and can begin their career in medicine quicker.


It is common to see acceptance rates for these programs ranging anywhere between 0.1% and 10%, and even though it may seem that such low acceptance chances would fall victim to luck, this is in fact not true—there are several steps high school students can take to significantly increase their chances of acceptance. Road to BSMD guides students carefully along these steps to build the strongest applications and the most confident applicants.
